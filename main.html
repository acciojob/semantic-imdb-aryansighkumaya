Kill is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film written and directed by Nikhil Nagesh Bhat. It is jointly produced by Karan Johar, Guneet Monga, Apoorva Mehta and Achin Jain. The film stars Lakshya, Raghav Juyal, Ashish Vidyarthi, Harsh Chhaya, Tanya Maniktala and Abhishek Chauhan.[3][4][5][6] Kill premiered at the Toronto International Film Festival on 7 September 2023, where it was first runner-up for the People's Choice Award: Midnight Madness.[7][8][9][10] It was also screened at the Tribeca Film Festival in June 2024.[11][12][13] Kill was theatrically released on 5 July 2024, and received positive reviews from critics.

Laksh Lalwani, known as Lakshya, is an Indian actor. He played the titular warrior in the period drama series Porus and has since starred in the action film Kill.

Frankly, my dear, I don't give a damn

I had the pleasure of seeing Kill at its world premier during the Toronto International Film Festival, as part of a Midnight Madness screening. Kill represents an impressive change of pace for Indian cinema. Yes, action movies continue to be a mainstay of the genre, but rarely do Indian films feel welcome along their East and Southeast Asian compatriots. If I could best describe this film, think the sincerity and raw emotion of Indian cinema with the quality choreography of The Raid, Oldboy, or any Tony Jaa film.
